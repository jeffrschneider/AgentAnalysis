Task Decomposition Matrix

For each skill from your Agentic Replica, decide how it should be handled in the future state.

Template
TASK DECOMPOSITION: [Role ID]

skills_assessment:
  - skill_id: [From Agentic Replica]
    skill_name: [Name]
    current_state:
      time_spent: [% or hours/week]
      judgment_required: [low | medium | high]
      error_rate: [If known]
      pain_points:
        - [What's frustrating or inefficient?]

    future_state:
      classification: [automate | augment | human_only]
      rationale: [Why this classification?]
      agent_role: [What the agent does, if any]
      human_role: [What the human does, if any]
      autonomy_level: [Full | Supervised | Collaborative | Directed]

# Summary counts
summary:
  automate: [count] skills, [%] of time
  augment: [count] skills, [%] of time
  human_only: [count] skills, [%] of time

Classification guide:

  • Automate: Low judgment, repetitive, clear rules, high volume
  • Augment: Medium judgment, benefits from AI assistance, human makes final call
  • Human-only: High judgment, ethical decisions, relationship-dependent, novel situations

Feasibility & Impact Assessment

Evaluate whether your reengineered design is practical and worth pursuing.

Template
FEASIBILITY ASSESSMENT: [Project Name]

technical_feasibility:
  ai_capability_fit:
    score: [1-5]
    notes: [Can current AI reliably do this?]
  data_availability:
    score: [1-5]
    notes: [Is training/reference data available?]
  integration_complexity:
    score: [1-5]
    notes: [How hard to connect to existing systems?]
  overall_technical: [average score]

organizational_feasibility:
  stakeholder_buy_in:
    score: [1-5]
    notes: [Will affected people support this?]
  change_magnitude:
    score: [1-5]
    notes: [How disruptive is the change?]
  skill_gaps:
    score: [1-5]
    notes: [What new skills are needed?]
  overall_organizational: [average score]

impact_projection:
  time_savings:
    current_hours: [hours/week or month]
    projected_hours: [hours/week or month]
    savings_percent: [%]
  quality_improvement:
    current_error_rate: [%]
    projected_error_rate: [%]
  scalability:
    current_capacity: [volume]
    projected_capacity: [volume]
  cost_estimate:
    implementation: [one-time cost]

    likelihood: [low | medium | high]
    impact: [low | medium | high]
    mitigation: [How to address]

recommendation: [proceed | pilot | defer | reject]

Complete Worked Example: Financial Analyst Reengineering

Continuing from the Agentic Replica example, here's how to reengineer the Financial Analyst role with AI agents.

1
Task Decomposition

Example
TASK DECOMPOSITION: FIN-ANALYST-001

skills_assessment:
  - skill_id: SKILL-001
    skill_name: Monthly Variance Analysis
    current_state:
      time_spent: 25%
      judgment_required: high
      error_rate: ~5% require rework
      pain_points:
        - Manual data gathering takes 2+ hours
        - Root cause research is repetitive
        - Commentary writing is time-consuming
    future_state:
      classification: augment
      rationale: Data gathering and initial analysis can be automated,
                  but variance interpretation requires human judgment
      agent_role: Gather data, flag variances, draft commentary
      human_role: Review analysis, refine commentary, approve
      autonomy_level: Collaborative

  - skill_id: SKILL-002
    skill_name: Weekly Cash Flow Reporting
    current_state:
      time_spent: 15%
      judgment_required: low
      error_rate: ~2%
      pain_points:
        - Tedious data extraction every week
        - Formatting is repetitive
    future_state:
      classification: automate
      rationale: Standard report, clear rules, low judgment
      agent_role: Generate complete report autonomously
      human_role: Spot-check monthly, handle exceptions
      autonomy_level: Supervised

  - skill_id: SKILL-003
    skill_name: Ad-hoc Analysis Requests
    current_state:
      time_spent: 20%
      judgment_required: high
      pain_points:
        - Unclear requirements from requesters
        - Similar analyses recreated from scratch
    future_state:
      classification: augment
      rationale: Agent can clarify requirements, retrieve similar
                  past analyses, draft initial approach
      agent_role: Clarify request, find similar analyses, draft approach
      human_role: Approve approach, execute complex analysis, present
      autonomy_level: Collaborative

  - skill_id: SKILL-005
    skill_name: Data Quality Monitoring
    current_state:
      time_spent: 10%
      judgment_required: low
      pain_points:
        - Manual checks are boring and error-prone
        - Issues discovered late in the process
    future_state:
      classification: automate
      rationale: Rule-based checks, continuous monitoring possible
      agent_role: Continuous monitoring, alert on anomalies
      human_role: Investigate alerts, resolve root causes
      autonomy_level: Full

  - skill_id: SKILL-006
    skill_name: Stakeholder Communication
    current_state:
      time_spent: 10%
      judgment_required: medium
    future_state:
      classification: human_only
      rationale: Relationship-dependent, political sensitivity
      agent_role: None (may draft routine updates)
      human_role: All stakeholder communication
      autonomy_level: N/A

summary:
  automate: 2 skills (SKILL-002, SKILL-005), 25% of time
  augment: 2 skills (SKILL-001, SKILL-003), 45% of time
  human_only: 2 skills (SKILL-004, SKILL-006), 30% of time

2
Agent Specification (Variance Analysis Agent)

Example
AGENT STORY: FPA-VARIANCE-001

As a Variance Analysis Agent,
triggered by monthly close completion notification,
I gather financial data, identify significant variances, and draft analysis,
so that analysts can focus on interpretation rather than data gathering.

Autonomy: Collaborative

# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────

trigger:
  type: message
  source: Accounting system or Slack #accounting channel
  conditions: Month-end close complete, GL data available
  examples:
    - "Slack: 'January books closed, GL ready for FP&A'"
    - "System event: NetSuite close status = complete"

behavior:
  type: workflow
  stages:
    - name: Data Gathering
      purpose: Extract GL data and budget from source systems
      transitions:
        - to: Variance Detection
          when: All data extracted successfully
        - to: Human Escalation
          when: Data extraction fails or data quality issues

    - name: Variance Detection
      purpose: Calculate variances, flag items exceeding thresholds
      transitions:
        - to: Root Cause Research
          when: Flagged variances identified
        - to: Report Assembly
          when: No significant variances (rare)

    - name: Root Cause Research
      purpose: Investigate likely causes using historical patterns
      transitions:
        - to: Commentary Drafting
          when: Research complete for all flagged items

    - name: Commentary Drafting
      purpose: Generate draft explanations for each variance
      transitions:
        - to: Human Review
          when: All drafts complete

    - name: Human Review
      purpose: Analyst reviews, edits, approves analysis
      transitions:
        - to: Report Assembly
          when: Analyst approves

    - name: Report Assembly
      purpose: Generate final formatted report

  planning: local

skills:
  - name: Financial Data Extraction
    domain: ERP systems and financial databases
    proficiencies:
      - Execute NetSuite saved searches and exports
      - Pull budget data from Adaptive Insights
      - Validate data completeness and reasonableness
    tools_used: [NetSuite MCP, Adaptive API]
    quality_bar: 100% of required data fields extracted accurately
    acquired: built_in

  - name: Variance Calculation
    domain: Financial analysis
    proficiencies:
      - Calculate budget vs actual variances by account
      - Apply materiality thresholds ($10K or 10%)
      - Identify month-over-month trends
    tools_used: [Internal calculation engine]
    quality_bar: Calculations match manual verification 100%
    acquired: built_in

  - name: Pattern Recognition
    domain: Financial trend analysis
    proficiencies:
      - Match current variances to historical patterns
      - Retrieve similar past variances and their explanations
      - Suggest likely root causes based on patterns
    tools_used: [Variance History Vector DB]
    quality_bar: Root cause suggestions relevant 80%+ of the time
    acquired: learned

  - name: Financial Writing
    domain: FP&A communication
    proficiencies:
      - Draft variance commentary in company style
      - Summarize complex issues clearly
      - Flag items needing CFO attention
    tools_used: [LLM]
    quality_bar: Drafts require <30% editing by analyst
    acquired: learned

tools:
  - name: NetSuite MCP
    purpose: GL data extraction, transaction drill-down
    permissions: read

  - name: Adaptive Insights API
    purpose: Budget and forecast data retrieval
    permissions: read

  - name: Variance History Vector DB
    purpose: Similar variance retrieval for pattern matching
    permissions: read, write

  - name: Report Generator
    purpose: Format and output final variance reports
    permissions: execute

memory:
  working:
    - Current month's extracted data
    - Flagged variances and draft commentary
  persistent:
    - name: Variance History
      type: vector
      purpose: Store past variances for pattern matching
      updates: append
  learning:
    - type: feedback_loop
      signal: Analyst edits to draft commentary

human_interaction:
  mode: in_the_loop
  checkpoints:
    - name: Analysis Review
      trigger: Draft analysis complete
      type: review
      timeout: Alert manager if no response in 24h
    - name: Material Variance Alert
      trigger: Any variance > $100K
      type: escalation
      timeout: Immediate notification
  escalation:
    conditions: Data extraction failure, unrecognized variance pattern,
                 confidence < 70%
    channel: Slack DM to assigned analyst

acceptance:
  functional:
    - Extracts all required data within 15 minutes of trigger
    - Identifies 100% of variances exceeding thresholds
    - Produces draft analysis by business day 6
  quality:
    - Draft commentary requires <30% analyst editing
    - Root cause suggestions relevant 80%+ of time
    - Zero calculation errors
  guardrails:
    - Never publish analysis without human approval
    - Never modify source system data
    - Never share analysis outside finance team

Feasibility Assessment

Example
FEASIBILITY ASSESSMENT: FP&A Variance Analysis Automation

technical_feasibility:
  ai_capability_fit:
    score: 4
    notes: Data extraction and pattern matching are strong AI use cases.
           Commentary drafting is feasible with current LLMs.
  data_availability:
    score: 5
    notes: 3 years of variance reports available for training.
           All source systems have APIs.
  integration_complexity:
    score: 3
    notes: NetSuite API well-documented. Adaptive requires custom
           connector. Report formatting needs template work.
  overall_technical: 4.0

organizational_feasibility:
  stakeholder_buy_in:
    score: 4
    notes: Analysts excited to reduce data gathering.
           CFO supportive of faster reporting.
  change_magnitude:
    score: 4
    notes: Workflow changes but core analyst role preserved.
           Shifts focus to interpretation vs data gathering.
  skill_gaps:
    score: 4
    notes: Analysts need training on reviewing AI output.
           IT needs agent management skills.
  overall_organizational: 4.0

impact_projection:
  time_savings:
    current_hours: 20 hours/month (variance analysis)
    projected_hours: 6 hours/month (review and refine)
    savings_percent: 70%
  quality_improvement:
    current_error_rate: 5% require rework
    projected_error_rate: 1% (calculation errors eliminated)
  scalability:
    current_capacity: 1 analyst handles 1 business unit
    projected_capacity: 1 analyst can oversee 3 business units
  cost_estimate:
    implementation: $75,000 (development + integration)
    ongoing: $2,000/month (API costs + maintenance)
    roi_timeline: 8 months

risks:
  - risk: AI misses unusual variance patterns
    likelihood: medium
    impact: high
    mitigation: Human review checkpoint, continuous learning from
               analyst corrections

  - risk: Commentary tone doesn't match company culture
    likelihood: medium
    impact: low
    mitigation: Fine-tune on historical reports, analyst editing phase

  - risk: Source system API changes break extraction
    likelihood: low
    impact: high
    mitigation: Monitoring, fallback to manual extraction,
               vendor relationship management

recommendation: proceed
rationale: Strong technical fit, organizational support, clear ROI.
           Start with variance analysis as pilot, expand to other
           FP&A workflows based on results.
